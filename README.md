# Welcome — Deep Learning, Fall 2026 👋

This is the **front door** for the Fall 2026 cohort of Deep Learning at the Hertie School Data Science Lab.

## Joining the course

1. Open a **[new issue](../../issues/new/choose)** → *"Join the course"*.
2. Your **GitHub handle is captured automatically** (you're the issue author — no typing, no faking).
3. Enter your **student ID** so we can match you to the enrolment roster. **Do not post your email** — it's already on the private roster.
4. An Action confirms you, matches you to the roster, invites you to the cohort, and provisions your assignment repos.

## Where things live

| What | Where |
| --- | --- |
| Syllabus · schedule · slides | the course **site** (link in the org profile) |
| Announcements | **Discussions → Announcements** |
| Your assignment repos | created for you in this org once you've joined |
